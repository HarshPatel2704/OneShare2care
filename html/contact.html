Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a donation?

You can make a donation by visiting our Donate page, selecting a campaign, choosing your donation amount, and completing the payment process. We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! Kindio is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible. You'll receive a tax receipt via email after your donation.

How can I track my donation's impact?

Each campaign page shows real-time progress and impact metrics. You can also receive regular updates about how your donation is making a difference in the community.

Can I set up a monthly donation?

You can choose to make a monthly recurring donation during the checkout process. You can cancel or modify your subscription at any time.
